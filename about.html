How I Got Here

It started in 2016, as a single mom selling life insurance.

I was a life insurance agent and a single mom.

Back then, the best way to sell insurance was getting in front of people after 5 p.m. and on weekends — exactly when my son needed me and daycare was closed.

Door-knocking was not going to work for my life.

So I found Meta ads. I realized I could use ads to get in front of the right people during the hours I could not leave home.

And it worked.

I started booking calls, writing policies, and other agents began asking how I was doing it.

At first, I built a course to teach them. But most of them did not want to learn how to run ads.

They wanted me to do it for them.

That is how the done-for-you side of Perfectly Made Marketing was born.
