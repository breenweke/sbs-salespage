How I got here

It started in 2016, as a single mom
selling life insurance.

I was a life insurance agent and a single mom. The best way to sell insurance back then was getting in front of people after 5pm and on weekends — exactly when my son needed me and daycare was closed. Door-knocking was not going to work for my life, so I found Meta ads. I could buy my way in front of the right people during the hours I could not leave home.

It worked. I started booking calls, writing policies, and other agents began asking how I was doing it. I built a course to teach them — but most of them did not want to learn it. They wanted me to do it for them. That is how the done-for-you side of this business was born.

But we kept running into the same problem — and that problem is the reason the Self-Booking System exists at all.

We were running lead form ads, and lead forms have a predictable failure pattern: leads would not pick up, or they would forget they opted in, or the agent would get rejected a few times and quietly stop following up. Leads coming in. Ad spend going out. No closes. So I went back to the drawing board and prayed about it. God showed me what I now call the Self-Booking System — a high-converting lead magnet, an immediate opportunity to book, and automated follow-up for everyone who was not ready in the moment.

My calendar started filling. My email list started growing. I could focus on what I did best and be with my family, knowing lead gen was happening whether I was online or not. That is what we now build for our clients — and the system is the system, no matter what industry they are in.

Is this a fit

Want results like this
for your business?

The Self-Booking System is not for everyone — and I would rather be honest about that up front than sell you on something that is not going to work.

We would love to work with you if

  • You are an established service provider — coach, consultant, agent, broker, practitioner, advisor
  • You have a proven offer you have already sold (not a concept, not an idea)
  • You can sustain a $20–$30/day minimum ad budget for at least 60 days (ideally $50+/day)
  • You are done trying to piece it together alone and you want someone to build the whole thing for you
  • You are ready to let go of hustle-based lead gen and let a system carry the weight

We are not the right fit if

  • You are looking for overnight results — this is a real system, not a hack
  • You need to be convinced that Meta ads work
  • You do not have a proven offer yet
  • You want to spend the lowest amount possible and expect the highest return
  • You are not ready to commit to running the system long enough to let it work
