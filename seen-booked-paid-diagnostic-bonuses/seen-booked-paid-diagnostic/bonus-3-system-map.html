01
Seen
Attract the buyer, not the browser
Optimized profile
Bio speaks to the problem. Link goes to lead magnet — not homepage.
Ad creative
Purpose-built video or image. Leads with symptom, not solution.
Ad copy + hook
First line names the exact situation. One clear CTA at the end.
Lead magnet
Specific enough to attract only the right person. Solves a named problem.
Audience targeting
Pixel installed. Warm custom audiences built. Retargeting layer active.
Stage outcome
The right person raises their hand
02
Booked
From opted-in to on the calendar — automatically
Opt-in page
Headline mirrors the ad. No navigation. One goal — the email address.
Thank you page
Booking link embedded. Highest-interest moment — do not waste it.
Booking page
Explains who the call is for and what they will walk away with.
Appointment setup
Confirmation email instant. Reminders at 48 hrs, 24 hrs, and 1 hr before call.
Follow-up emails
Email 1 within 30 min of opt-in. Emails 2–3 at 24 hrs and 48 hrs.
Stage outcome
The call is on the calendar
03
Paid
Nurture, re-engage, and convert the long game
Welcome sequence
Picks up after follow-up ends. Proof story. Trust building. Booking invitation.
Nurture emails
Weekly. Value, story, proof. Keeps your name in front of warm leads.
Re-engagement
Targets leads gone quiet. Direct question. Booking link. No pressure.
Call reminders
Periodic direct invitation to book. Calendar link visible. Sent to full list.
Discovery call
Lead arrives warm. System built the trust. Your job: confirm the fit.
Stage outcome
The lead becomes a client
The rule: Build Seen before Booked. Build Booked before Paid. Build Booked before running ads. Every stage depends on the one before it — a working Seen stage cannot rescue a missing Booked stage.
