Bree Nweke

Wife Mom of 3 Founder Speaker Methodology Creator
Teaching service providers to build calendars that fill themselves.

Bree Nweke is a wife, mom of three, and the founder of Perfectly Made Marketing. She is the creator of The Self-Booking System — a Meta-ads-driven methodology that helps established service providers build a calendar that fills itself, without posting daily, chasing leads in DMs, or being the engine of their own business.

Bree began her career in 2016 as a single mom selling life insurance, where she discovered Meta ads as the only way to reach her ideal clients during the hours she could not be in front of them. After years of refining what she now calls the Self-Booking System, she has personally trained 81 students through her courses and built done-for-you installs for life insurance agents, mortgage brokers, business funding experts, psychologists, therapists, and faith-led coaches and consultants.

Bree speaks with the calm directness of someone who has done the work — no hype, no shaming, no marketing-bro energy. She brings nine years of paid-ads pattern recognition, the lived experience of building a business that supports a family, and a stewardship-first approach to entrepreneurship that resonates with audiences who care as much about how they grow as what they grow.

Bree loves long road trips with her family, unhurried mornings, and unapologetically deep conversations about why most marketing advice is either wrong or incomplete.
