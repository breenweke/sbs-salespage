Here's How It Works

From kickoff call to final report.

Step 1

We kick things off.

We'll start with a simple kickoff call so I can learn more about your business, your offer, your ideal client, your lead magnet, and your goals.

You'll share the lead magnet you want to promote, along with your landing page, thank-you page, brand details, and any access needed to set up your ads.

This is where we get clear on the goal:

How do we get more right-fit people onto your email list and into your world in the next 30 days?

Step 2

We review your lead magnet + funnel.

Before we start sending paid traffic to your lead magnet, I'll review the opt-in path.

That may include your:

  • Lead magnet title and promise
  • Opt-in page
  • Thank-you page
  • Confirmation or delivery process
  • Next step after someone joins your list

The goal is to make sure nothing obvious is working against conversions before we start driving traffic.

You'll need at least one lead magnet ready to promote — or a lead magnet idea if you want us to create it for you. If you have two lead magnets, we can test them against each other to see which one your audience responds to best.

Step 3

I create the ad copy + creative.

For your campaign, I'll create two versions of ad copy and creative per lead magnet.

That gives us different angles to test so we can see what message and visual direction your audience responds to best.

This helps us answer important questions like:

  • Which hook gets attention?
  • Which message creates interest?
  • Which creative gets people to stop scrolling?
  • Which lead magnet feels most compelling to your audience?

No more staring at a blank document wondering what your ad should say. I'll handle the copy and creative direction so your campaign is built strategically from the start.

Step 4

I research + set up your audiences.

We'll test up to three targeting groups so we can see which audience gives you the strongest results.

This is a big part of the value of the sprint.

Because at the end, you will not just know whether your lead magnet can get leads. You'll also have a clearer picture of which audience responded best.

That means you can keep building your list with more confidence instead of wondering who to target next.

Step 5

I set up the campaign.

Once the ads are ready and approved by you, I'll set up the campaign in your ad account.

This includes:

  • Campaign setup
  • Ad set setup
  • Audience setup
  • Ad copy and creative upload
  • Basic tracking check
  • Pixel setup or review
  • Budget setup
  • Launch preparation

You'll pay ad spend directly to Meta.

Step 6

I manage + optimize for 30 days.

Once your campaign is live, I'll monitor and optimize it during the 30-day sprint.

I'll look at what's working, what is not working, and where we should adjust.

This may include:

  • Turning off underperforming ads
  • Shifting budget toward stronger results
  • Comparing opt-ins
  • Reviewing audiences
  • Checking cost per lead
  • Making recommendations based on the data

The goal is not just to "run ads."

The goal is to find the clearest path to growing your list with warm, aligned leads.

Step 7

You get a final report + next-step plan.

At the end of the 30 days, you'll receive a simple results review.

You'll know:

  • Which lead magnet performed best
  • Which ad copy worked best
  • Which creative worked best
  • Which audience responded best
  • What your cost per lead was
  • How many new leads joined your list
  • What I recommend you do next

So instead of ending the sprint with confusion, you'll end with clarity. You'll know what to keep running, what to turn off, and what your next best move should be.
