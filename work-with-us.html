Questions + Answers

The things people ask us most.

Is this only for Christian coaches and consultants?

Our agency is built to serve Christian coaches, consultants, course creators, strategists, educators, and service-based business owners with proven offers.

If your business is faith-led and you want strategic Meta ads support with the option for funnel building, you're in the right place.

If you're not Christian, that's okay too! Feel free to apply, but just know that we praise Jesus over here and will not shy away from mentioning Him.

What is the difference between Meta Ads Management and Meta Ads Management + Funnel Build?

Meta Ads Management is for you if you already have a proven offer and a funnel, sales page, booking page, webinar, challenge, lead magnet, or sales process ready for traffic.

Meta Ads Management + Funnel Build is for you if you want us to manage the ads, but you also need the funnel built or cleaned up first. This may include a landing page, opt-in page, thank-you page, email sequence, booking flow, tripwire funnel, self-liquidating offer funnel, or full Self-Booking System.

Do I need to have a proven offer before working with you?

Yes. Our full ads management services are best for Christian coaches, consultants, course creators, and service-based business owners who already have a proven product or service.

That means people have paid for it, you know who it helps, and you have testimonials, reviews, client wins, or results you can point to.

Ads can help amplify a proven offer, but they are not meant to validate a brand-new idea from scratch.

How much should I expect to spend on ads?

We recommend being prepared to spend at least $50–$100/day depending on the campaign goal.

For testing, $50/day may be enough to start gathering data. Ideally, we like to see clients closer to $100/day, especially if the goal is sales, booked calls, challenge registrations, webinar registrations, or scaling a proven funnel.

Is ad spend included in your monthly management fee?

No. Your management fee is paid to Perfectly Made Marketing for strategy, setup, creative, campaign management, testing, optimization, communication, and reporting.

Your ad spend is paid directly to Meta.

If your monthly ad spend goes above $10,000/month, a 10% ad spend fee applies.

What does the 3-month minimum mean?

We require a minimum commitment because ads need time to test, learn, and optimize.

Month 1 is usually focused on strategy, setup, creative, campaign buildout, tracking, and funnel creation if needed.

Months 2 and 3 give us time to review the data, test messaging and creative, optimize the campaign, and make smarter decisions based on what your audience is actually doing.

After the initial commitment, your agreement moves to month-to-month and can be canceled with at least 30 days written notice.

An additional month is required for the funnel build because the first month is heavily focused on strategy, building, setup, and launching your funnel — and Months 2-4 are where we test, optimize, and improve based on the ad data.

What is included in the funnel build?

Your first month includes one funnel build. Depending on your goal, that may include:

  • A lead magnet funnel.
  • A Self-Booking System funnel.
  • A webinar or challenge registration funnel.
  • A tripwire or self-liquidating offer funnel.
  • An application funnel.
  • A booking page or booking flow.
  • Email nurture or sales sequence.
  • Basic automations.
  • Tracking setup or review.

Additional funnel builds can be requested at an additional cost.

Can you build more than one funnel?

Yes, but the first-month funnel build includes one funnel.

If you need multiple funnels — for example, a lead magnet funnel and a webinar funnel, or a tripwire funnel and a Self-Booking System — we can quote the additional build separately. Also be advised that more ad spend will be required per funnel you want us to build.

What platforms do you build the funnel in?

We prefer to build in GoHighLevel or Systeme.io.

If you do not already use one of those platforms, you can use our custom CRM for free while you are an active client.

If you decide to stop working with us and want to continue using the CRM, it is $97/month.

Will you write the ad copy and create the ad creative?

Yes. Ad copy and creative are included in our Meta Ads Management services.

We handle campaign strategy, audience research, targeting, setup, ad copy, creative, testing, monitoring, feedback, optimization, weekly check-ins, and monthly reporting.

Will you manage my funnel too, or only the ads?

For Meta Ads Management clients, we manage the ads and provide strategy feedback/suggestions based on what we see happening in the funnel and you or a member of your team will make the corrections.

For Meta Ads Management + Funnel Build clients, we build or clean up one funnel before or alongside the ads so the campaign has a stronger path to send traffic into.

Can you help me if I want to grow my email list instead of book calls?

Yes. We can support campaigns designed to grow your email list, book sales calls, fill a challenge, promote a webinar, sell a course, drive traffic to a tripwire, retarget warm audiences, or support a launch or evergreen funnel.

The strategy depends on your offer, audience, funnel, and goal. If you only want us to focus on helping you bring in more warm leads and grow your email list without the 3 month commitment, our 30 Day Leads on Demand sprint might be better for you. You can learn more about it here.

Do you guarantee leads, sales, booked calls, or revenue?

No. We do not guarantee specific leads, sales, booked calls, revenue, or return on ad spend.

Results depend on your offer, audience, funnel, messaging, ad spend, market conditions, follow-up, and sales process.

What we do provide is strategy, setup, campaign management, creative testing, optimization, reporting, and a full-path approach so your ads have a stronger chance of performing well.
