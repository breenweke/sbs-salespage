The Whole Path Matters

Your Ads Might Not Be the Only Problem

A lot of business owners assume that if their ads are not performing, the ad itself must be broken.

But your ads are only one part of the path.

1.

Someone sees the ad.

2.

They click.

3.

They land on a page.

4.

They decide whether to opt in, book, buy, apply, or leave.

5.

Then they either receive follow-up — or they disappear.

That means your results can be affected by what happens before the click, during the click, and after the click.

Inside this audit, I review both sides:

1

Your Ads

The campaign setup, strategy, targeting, creative, copy, numbers, and tracking.

2

Your Funnel

The offer, landing page, thank-you page, sales path, and email follow-up connected to the campaign.

Because if your ads and funnel are not working together, you may keep changing the wrong thing.
